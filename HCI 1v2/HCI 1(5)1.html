Number Pairs and Doubles

Number Pairs (6-10)

Number pairs are two numbers that work together to make another number. For example, 4 + 2 makes 6, so 4 and 2 are a pair for 6.

Let's find the number pairs for 6, 7, 8, 9, and 10!

Number Pairs for 6

6:

  • 1 + 5 = 6
  • 2 + 4 = 6
  • 3 + 3 = 6

Number Pairs for 7

Know Doubles to at Least Double 5

Doubling means adding a number to itself. For example, double 2 means 2 + 2. Doubling is helpful for quick mental math. Once you know your doubles you can solve problems faster!

  • Double of 1: 1 + 1 = 2
  • Double of 2: 2 + 2 = 4
  • Double of 3: 3 + 3 = 6
  • Double of 4: 4 + 4 = 8
  • Double of 5: 5 + 5 = 10
