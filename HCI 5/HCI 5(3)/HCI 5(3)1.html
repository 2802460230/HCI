Data Collection and Analysis

Let’s learn how to collect, organize, and analyze data to answer questions!
By collecting data, organizing it into tables or graphs, and analyzing it, we can draw conclusions and make informed decisions.
Step 1: Collecting Data
To collect data, decide what information you need to answer a question.

Example: If you want to know the favorite fruit in your class, ask everyone their choice and write it down.

Put the information into a table, chart, or graph to make it easier to understand.

Step 3: Analyzing Data
Look at the organized data to answer your question.

Example: If apples have the most tally marks, they are the most popular fruit. Draw conclusions like: “Apples are the class's favorite fruit.”
