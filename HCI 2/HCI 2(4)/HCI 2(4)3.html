Know Units of Time

What are Units of Time?
Units of time help us measure how long something takes or when something happens. We use seconds, minutes, hours, days, weeks, months, and years to tell time.
Seconds:
Seconds are very short. It takes about 1 second to clap your hands once.

Example: It takes about 1 second to blink your eyes once.

Quiz: How many seconds are in a minute?





Minutes:
A minute is longer than a second. There are 60 seconds in 1 minute.

Example: Brushing your teeth takes about 2 minutes.

Quiz: How many minutes are in an hour?





Hours:
An hour is much longer than a minute. There are 60 minutes in 1 hour.

Example: It takes about 1 hour to watch a cartoon.

Quiz: How many hours are in a day?





Days:
A day is the time from when the sun rises to when it rises again. There are 24 hours in a day.

Example: We usually sleep at night and wake up in the morning.
